Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Marien ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SkyDodge mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Information collected automatically

We do not collect any personal information directly. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain data automatically:

  • Device identifiers (such as IDFA) for advertising purposes
  • Advertising data (ad interactions, impressions) for serving relevant ads
  • Game progress data stored locally on your device (scores, unlocked items, settings)

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any contact information
  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not collect health, financial, or biometric data

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements (interstitial and rewarded video ads). AdMob may collect and process data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

AdMob may use device identifiers and advertising data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent preferences. You can manage your ad preferences through the "Manage ad consent" option in the App's Settings.

Apple Game Center

The App uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center data is managed by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple StoreKit

The App offers an optional in-app purchase ("Remove Ads"). Transactions are processed by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

All game progress (scores, coins, unlocked skins, settings) is stored locally on your device in a JSON file. This data is not transmitted to any server and is not backed up to any cloud service by us.

4. Advertising & Tracking

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. Before any tracking occurs, we request your consent through:

  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — for GDPR/EEA consent
  • Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — for IDFA access

You may decline tracking at any time. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized.

You may also purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase to remove all advertising from the App.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no data to retain or delete on our end. Locally stored game data can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via ATT prompt or Settings > "Manage ad consent")
  • Withdraw consent at any time

For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's data controls.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
